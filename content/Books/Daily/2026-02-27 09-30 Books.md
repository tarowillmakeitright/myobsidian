## Legacy / timeless must-reads (high-value re-reads)

5) **Neuromancer** — *William Gibson*
- **Why it matters now:** Cyberpunk DNA for modern AI/cyber discourse; still the best lens on network power and digital identity.
- **Reference:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neuromancer

6) **The Left Hand of Darkness** — *Ursula K. Le Guin*
- **Why it matters now:** Enduring SF benchmark for politics, culture, and gender; remains sharper than most “future society” fiction.
- **Reference:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Left_Hand_of_Darkness

7) **Gödel, Escher, Bach** — *Douglas Hofstadter*
- **Why it matters now:** Foundational for thinking about mind, computation, recursion, and AI interpretability mindsets.
- **Reference:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/G%C3%B6del,_Escher,_Bach

8) **The Cuckoo’s Egg** — *Clifford Stoll*
- **Why it matters now:** Still one of the clearest narratives of real-world intrusion investigation and operational persistence.
- **Reference:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Cuckoo%27s_Egg_(book)
