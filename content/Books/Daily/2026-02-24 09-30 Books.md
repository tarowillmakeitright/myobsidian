## Legacy / Timeless Must-Reads

5) **Neuromancer** — *William Gibson* (1984)
- **Why it matters now:** Still the canonical map of cyberspace, corporate power, and hacker culture; foundational context for today’s AI + cyber discourse.
- **Reference:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neuromancer

6) **The Cuckoo’s Egg** — *Cliff Stoll* (1989)
- **Why it matters now:** Real-world intrusion detection, patient investigation, and operational security mindset—evergreen security craft.
- **Reference:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Cuckoo%27s_Egg_(book)

7) **The Left Hand of Darkness** — *Ursula K. Le Guin* (1969)
- **Why it matters now:** Gold-standard SF for social systems, gender, and anthropology—excellent for broadening speculative reasoning.
- **Reference:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Left_Hand_of_Darkness

8) **Gödel, Escher, Bach** — *Douglas Hofstadter* (1979)
- **Why it matters now:** Deep intuition for recursion, representation, and mind/machine questions—still highly relevant in the LLM era.
- **Reference:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/G%C3%B6del,_Escher,_Bach
